Overview

FinanceApp is a personal finance tracker for Android. This policy explains how your data is handled. The short version: it isn't — everything stays on your device.

Data collection

We collect nothing.

All information you enter — accounts, transactions, budgets, and categories — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. No data is ever sent to us or any third party.

FinanceApp has no backend. There are no servers receiving your information.
Network access

The app is fully offline.

FinanceApp makes zero network requests. It does not connect to the internet, cloud services, or any external APIs — ever.

Local storage

Your data lives on your phone.

Financial data is stored in a private app database accessible only to FinanceApp. It is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Data export

Export is in your hands.

You may export your data as a JSON file to any location on your device. You are solely responsible for how exported files are stored or shared.

Third-party services

None.

FinanceApp uses no third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any other purpose.

Children

FinanceApp is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
