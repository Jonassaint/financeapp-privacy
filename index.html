1. Overview

FinanceApp is a personal finance tracker for Android. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored.

2. Data We Collect

FinanceApp does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — accounts, transactions, budgets, and categories — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database.

3. No Internet Access

The app operates entirely offline. It makes no network requests and does not connect to any servers, cloud services, or third-party APIs.

4. Data Storage

Your financial data is stored locally on your Android device in a private app database. It is not accessible to other apps and is deleted when you uninstall FinanceApp.

5. Data Export

FinanceApp allows you to export your data as a JSON file to a location of your choosing on your device. You are solely responsible for how you store or share exported files.

6. Third-Party Services

FinanceApp does not use any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any other purpose.

7. Children's Privacy

FinanceApp is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
